Schools - Context

Monitoring how well we are delivering a service really means paying attention to the results we want to see.
We measure outcome, not activity.

For schools, those outcomes are:

  • More children attending school, improving their chances of getting a qualification that leads to a fulfulling career.
  • Children from poorer or disadvantaged backgrounds getting fair access to opportunities.
  • Schools are creating not just academic learning, but a safe and supportive environment to learn other life skills.
